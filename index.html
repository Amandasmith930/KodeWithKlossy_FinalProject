about us

Self-care is a broad term that encompasses anything you can do to take care of yourself. Although we have no problem taking care of our friends, family, plants, and pets, it can be difficult at times to know your worth and know that you need to slow down. Take this time NOW to meditate, vent to us, and calm down. We’re glad you’re here. <3

Feel like you never have time to yourself? Want to have a place to destress and unwind? Well, Y.O.U is the place for you! Life presents everyday struggles and anxiety that can sometimes make you feel like there is no calm place to turn. We decided to create a place to turn. Take time for yourself; learn how to unwind in a virtual setting with resources, facts, advice, and fun!
