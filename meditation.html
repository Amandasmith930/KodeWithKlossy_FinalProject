Defined by Merriam-Webster, meditation means “to engage in mental exercise (such as concentration on one’s breathing or repetition of a mantra) for the purpose of reaching a heightened level of spiritual awareness.” In other words, it is taking charge of our mind. We can meditate to become more aware of ourselves and everything around us. Meditation has innumerable physical and mental heath benefits, but don’t just take it from us. Click here to learn about 12 science-based benefits of meditation.

Breathing is a simple way to calm the mind and return to a state of mental clarity. Focusing on your breathing can reduce tension and anxiety. There are many other positive health benefits. Click here to learn more about the true importance of breathing.
